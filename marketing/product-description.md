# ContractFlow — Product Description

## The Problem

Legal teams at growing companies waste 40% of their time on manual contract processes:
- Copying and pasting from Word templates
- Emailing PDFs back and forth for signatures
- Manually tracking contract expiry dates in spreadsheets
- Waiting days for multi-level approval chains
- Having no visibility into contract risk until problems occur

This costs companies an average of $50,000–$200,000 per year in lost productivity, missed renewals, and legal exposure.

---

## The Solution

**ContractFlow** is an AI-powered contract lifecycle management platform that automates the entire contract journey—from drafting to signature to renewal.

### Core Capabilities

**📝 AI Contract Drafting**
Generate production-ready contracts in seconds. Input your parameters—parties, jurisdiction, term length—and ContractFlow's GPT-4o integration produces a polished, customizable draft. Supports NDAs, MSAs, SOWs, employment agreements, and more.

**🔍 AI Risk Analysis**
Every contract gets a 0-100 risk score before signing. Our AI identifies problematic clauses, liability exposure, and non-standard terms—flagging issues like unlimited indemnity, unilateral modification rights, and unfavorable IP assignments.

**✍️ E-Signatures**
Built-in electronic signature workflow with real-time status tracking. Multi-party signing with automatic contract activation when all parties sign. Full audit trail for legal compliance.

**⚡ Approval Workflows**
Configure multi-step approval chains: legal → finance → CEO. Parallel or sequential approvals. Automatic escalation and deadline tracking.

**📊 Contract Intelligence**
Analytics dashboard showing contract value at risk, expiry calendar, status distribution, and risk score trends. Never miss a renewal deadline again.

**🔒 Enterprise Security**
Row-level security on all data. SOC 2-ready audit trail. Role-based access (admin/manager/member). Invite-only team management.
