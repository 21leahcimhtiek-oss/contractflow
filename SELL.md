## Sales Objections & Responses

**"We already use DocuSign"**
DocuSign is great for e-signature, but ContractFlow is a full lifecycle platform — AI drafting, risk scoring, approval workflows, and version control — not just signing. Many customers use both.

**"Security concerns with AI"**
All contract content stays in your Supabase instance. We use OpenAI API (not training data). SOC 2 audit trail, RLS-enforced data isolation, and full encryption at rest.

**"We need enterprise features"**
Our Enterprise plan at $499/mo includes SSO, unlimited contracts, custom workflows, dedicated support, and SLA guarantees. We also offer white-label for resellers.
